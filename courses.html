Course 1

Aadi Kautilya was started in 2010 by a group of committed IITians in Sector 62, Noida.
During the last 6 years we have had the privilege of coaching several hundred students. For a brief period in 2012 we had a strategic alliance with Pie Education, one of the established pan-Indian coaching institutes for IIT-JEE of the time.

Aadi Kautilya, named after the legendary teacher statesman who was the moving force behind the Grand Mauryan Empire, prides itself on the quality of education it provides.
