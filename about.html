About Us

Aadi Kautilya was started in 2010 by a group of committed IITians in Sector - 62, Noida.
During the last 6 years we have had the privilege of coaching several hundred students.

For a brief period in 2012 we had a strategic alliance with Pie Education, one of the established pan-Indian coaching institutes for IIT-JEE of the time.
Aadi Kautilya, named after the legendary teacher statesman who was the moving force behind the Grand Mauryan Empire, prides itself on the quality of education it provides.




Our Philosophy

Every child has it in him to succeed.
God does not discriminate. Every human child is born with the qualities necessary to do well. How he / she is trained to use them separates the winners.

Different children have differing needs.
It has been borne out by the latest research on multiple intelligences. The theory states that different children develop different abilities. Some are good with logic. A few others might possess a more developed sense of space while another group of children might have a keener ear for rhythms. This makes for adaptive learning where the coach recognizes the student's innate abilities and orients his training around the same.

You don't have to work hard to score better.
Any one who finds the work "hard" is obviously not enjoying the stuff. It is difficult to put your heart into something you do not like. A good coach seeks to make training fun.

Quality education need not be unnecessarily costly or distant.
The Japanese made this into a basic diktat of their management. Quality, in the long run actually costs less. Applies equally well to education.

Studying is fun.
Physics (or for that matter chemistry or math) when properly handled can be fascinating as evidenced by the success of Hollywood movies like Interstellar and Gravity.
